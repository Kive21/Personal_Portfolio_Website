About Me.

Here, you'll delve deeper into the story behind the name. I am a dynamic individual driven by a passion for innovation, community empowerment, and continuous learning.

Born and raised in Kenya, my journey has been marked by a relentless pursuit of knowledge and a commitment to making a difference. Armed with a Bachelor's Degree in Project Planning and Management from Moi University, I have actively sought opportunities to apply my skills and expertise in practical settings, from supporting agricultural initiatives to developing innovative tech solutions.

Throughout my academic and professional endeavors, I have embraced challenges with enthusiasm, honed my leadership skills, and forged meaningful connections within my community and beyond. Whether it's through my involvement in projects like the Agri-Rays Project Manager at ENACTUS or my role as Vice Chairperson of the Kenya Red Cross Annex Chapter at Moi University, I am dedicated to driving positive change and inspiring others to do the same.

Beyond my achievements and accolades, I am defined by my values of integrity, resilience, and a deep-seated belief in the power of collaboration. As you navigate through this page, I invite you to join me on this journey of growth, exploration, and impact. Together, let's embark on a path towards a brighter future for ourselves and our communities.
